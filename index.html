Platform overview

Three data layers that close the gap between retail and institutional.

01

Real-time market data

Live quote feeds, level-two order book depth, options chain visualisation, and after-hours price tracking across North American exchanges. Streaming alerts trigger on user-defined price levels, volume spikes, unusual options activity, and short interest changes. Portfolio tracking integrates brokerage account balances via read-only API connections and reconciles positions in real time.

02

Fundamental research engine

Structured fundamental data covering ten years of income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements for every publicly traded equity on TSX, NYSE, and NASDAQ. Earnings estimates, analyst price targets, revenue surprises, and EPS revision histories are displayed in comparable formats that allow side-by-side sector analysis without requiring the user to open multiple data sources.

03

Screener and discovery

A multi-factor equity screener allowing users to filter the full North American market by over 200 financial and technical criteria including P/E ratio ranges, revenue growth thresholds, insider ownership percentages, dividend yield bands, and technical pattern triggers. Screener results export to watchlists and trigger ongoing alerts as screener conditions are met or broken by individual equities.
